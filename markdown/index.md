## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need to know calculus for this course?

No! We focus on linear algebra concepts that don't require calculus. The most complex operation is multiplication and addition.

### Q: What is a "feature vector" and why does it matter?

A feature vector is simply a list of numbers describing one item (like a customer). It matters because ALL machine learning models take feature vectors as input. Understanding vectors = understanding how ML works.

### Q: What's the difference between "conversion" and "churn"?

**Conversion** = when someone does what you wanted (like buying after an email). **Churn** = when someone leaves (stops buying, cancels subscription). Both are outcomes we predict using vectors!

### Q: Which lesson is most important for machine learning?

Lesson 4: Dot Product is the foundation of almost every ML prediction. If you only have time for one lesson, make it that one!

### Q: Can I skip the cross product lesson?

Yes, if you're only interested in tabular/spreadsheet data. Cross products are mainly used in 3D graphics and robotics, not typical customer analytics.
